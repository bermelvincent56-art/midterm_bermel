About This Project

My Classroom Availability Monitoring System (CAMS) is a web-based platform that allows administrators, faculty, and students to efficiently manage and view classroom usage within the school. It provides real-time updates on which classrooms are available, occupied, or under maintenance. Administrators can manage schedules, classrooms, and users; faculty can view their assigned schedules and request reservations; while students can log in to check room availability without making changes. CAMS improves transparency, prevents scheduling conflicts, and ensures better utilization of school facilities.
